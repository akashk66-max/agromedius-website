Essential Crop Details

Mangoes

Known for their sweet, juicy flesh and vibrant aroma. Harvested in summer.

**Growth Period:** 3-5 years from planting to first fruit.

**Peak Season:** April - August

Rice

A staple food crop grown extensively in India.

**Growth Period:** 3-6 months (depending on variety).

**Peak Season:** Varies by region, typically Kharif (June-Nov) and Rabi (Nov-April)

Potatoes

Versatile and widely cultivated root vegetable.

**Growth Period:** 2-4 months.

**Peak Season:** October - February
